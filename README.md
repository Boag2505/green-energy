# GreenEnergy RWA Hub

**Project Title**

GreenEnergy RWA Hub - Decentralized Solar Yield Fund

**Project Description**

GreenEnergy RWA Hub is a decentralized smart contract platform designed to tokenize real-world rooftop solar projects. Built using Soroban on the Stellar blockchain, it enables transparent micro-investments, automates the distribution of monthly electricity yields using stablecoins, and ensures an immutable, trustless record of ownership and dividend claims.

**Project Vision**

The vision of GreenEnergy RWA Hub is to democratize green energy investments. By eliminating traditional intermediaries, it allows retail investors to fund sustainable energy infrastructure while receiving automated, highly precise dividend payouts. This guarantees transparent cash flow management, lowers the barrier to entry for green investments, and fosters environmental sustainability.

**Key Features**

* **Asset Tokenization:** Admins can mint digital shares (tokens) representing fractional ownership of a physical solar energy plant.
* **Automated Yield Distribution:** Smart contract logic automatically calculates exactly how much yield an investor is owed based on their exact holding percentage and the total dividend pool.
* **Anti-Double Claim Mechanism:** Immutable on-chain tracking ensures users can only claim their rightful, up-to-date dividends without errors or duplicate withdrawals.
* **Transparent Yield Deposits:** Project owners can securely deposit monthly electricity revenues into the decentralized contract pool.
* **Micro-Payment Efficiency:** Leverages Stellar's low transaction fees to make fractional dividend payouts practical, even for small investors.
* **Access Control:** Admin-restricted token minting and revenue depositing, with user-restricted dividend claiming.

**Usage Instructions**

* **Initialize:** Deploy the contract and assign the admin address and total token supply (e.g., 10,000 shares).
* **Mint Shares:** Admin distributes tokens to investor wallets representing their ownership stake in the solar project.
* **Deposit Yield:** Project owner deposits monthly electricity revenue into the contract.
* **Claim Yield:** Investors interact with the contract to instantly calculate and receive their dividends directly to their wallets.
* **Query:** Anyone can query the total token supply and total dividends deposited for complete financial transparency.

**Future Scope**

* **Interactive Web Frontend:** Develop a responsive, user-friendly dashboard for investors to track real-time yields and manage their portfolios.
* **KYC & Compliance Authorization:** Utilize Stellar's built-in authorization flags and trustlines to ensure only verified investors can hold tokens, complying with real-world security regulations.
* **IoT Integration:** Connect smart electricity meters directly to the blockchain via off-chain oracles to automatically trigger yield deposits based on real-time kilowatt-hour (kWh) production.
* **Secondary Market:** Implement a trading mechanism or liquidity pool for investors to easily buy and sell their solar shares.

**Technology Stack**

* Rust and Soroban SDK for secure, robust smart contract development.
* Stellar blockchain for decentralized, high-speed, and low-cost micro-transactions.
